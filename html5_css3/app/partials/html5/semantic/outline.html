• The outline of a web page is the structure of the document
  • In HTML4, the outline algorithm was only based on the heading elements (h1, ..., h6)
  • In HTML5, the outline algorithm takes also in consideration the new semantic tags we have seen before ( body, section, article, aside, footer, header et nav)
  • The use of these element implies a new subsection in the outline
<h1>Welcome</h1> <section> <h1>About HTML5</h1> <p>So fine...</p> <section> <h1>Better that HTML4?</h1> <p>Yes sure...</p> </section> </section> <footer> <section> <h1>Contact</h1> <p>Feel free to ask your questions</p> </section> <section> <h1>About us</h1> <p>worldline</p> </section> </footer> 1.
