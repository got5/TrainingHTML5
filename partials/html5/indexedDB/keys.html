• Keys : Unique identifier
    • Strategy :
      • autoincrement (key is not part of the data !)
      • keyPath (identify data from a property)
      • keyPath + autoincrement
        • In case your key is not unique, it will use an extra field for the key
//... //use the email field as key newVersion.createObjectStore('EmailKey',{keyPath:'email'}); //or newVersion.createObjectStore('keyPathAuto',{keyPath:'id',autoIncrement:true}); //...
